Who We Are

V Capital Dubai is a private real estate intelligence and advisory platform operated by Vikraant K Parcha, based in Dubai, UAE. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through vcapital.ae.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our enquiry forms, including:

  • Name — to address you personally in our response
  • Phone number — to contact you via WhatsApp
  • Email address — to send your requested investment brief
  • Message content — to understand your investment objective

We also collect anonymised engagement data automatically — scroll depth, pages viewed, time on page, and session duration. This data contains no personally identifiable information and is used solely for improving the website's content.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry within 24 hours
  • Send the investment brief you requested
  • Follow up on your Dubai real estate objectives if you have indicated interest

We do not use your contact information for unsolicited marketing, and we do not share it with any third parties, developers, or agencies.

Data Storage

Form submissions are stored securely in a private Google Sheets document accessible only to V Capital. Data is retained for up to 24 months or until you request deletion.

Cookies

We use first-party cookies and localStorage to remember your cookie consent preference and improve your browsing experience. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You may accept or decline non-essential cookies via the cookie banner on your first visit.
